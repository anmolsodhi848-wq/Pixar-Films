# Pixar-Films
Built interactive Tableau dashboards using an Excel movie dataset to analyze film ratings, box office performance, audience scores, and profitability metrics.

**Project Objective**
To evaluate the relationships between movie ratings, budgets, revenues, and audience engagement by developing comprehensive Tableau visualizations.

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
1. Average IMDB Score – Measures overall audience rating quality across films.
2. Average Rotten Tomatoes Score – Indicates critic sentiment and review strength.
3. Total Worldwide Box Office Revenue – Represents overall financial success of movies globally.
4. Profit Margin (%) – Evaluates the profitability of each film relative to its budget.
5. Return on Investment (ROI) – Measures how efficiently each film converted budget into revenue.
6. Average Budget – Tracks spending trends and production cost levels.
7. Total Box Office (US & Canada + Other Markets) – Shows revenue distribution across regions.
8. IMDB Rating Counts – Reflects audience engagement and rating volume.
9. Rotten Tomatoes Rating Counts – Shows critic participation and review volume.
10. Average Run Time – Helps identify patterns in film duration across categories.
